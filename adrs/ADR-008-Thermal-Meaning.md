# ADR-008: Thermal Meaning

The word **quantum** in "quantum monad" is metaphorical unless an exact quantum-mechanical semantics is separately defined and verified.

**Energy annotations do NOT establish physical quantum computation.**

**Implementation**:
- State carries a refinement proof
- Bind composes proofs while scaling energy by φ^(-i)
- Energy is represented symbolically (golden ratio, exact algebraic structure)
- No analytic convergence claimed without rigorous domain and limit definition

**Forbidden**:
- Claiming quantum speedup without proof
- Using decimal 1.618 as φ in proof-relevant code
- Eliding analytic convergence to "just works"
